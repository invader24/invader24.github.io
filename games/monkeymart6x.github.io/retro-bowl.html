About Retro Bowl

Retro Bowl is a classic-style American football game that mixes team management with exciting on-field action. Play and conquer glory.

How to play Retro Bowl game

You play as both the head coach and quarterback of your team. So you must do a variety of tasks. Manage your roster, upgrade your staff, keepe your players happy, and most importantly, win games. There's a lot to do. If you’re just starting out, focus on building a strong quarterback and wide receiver combo. Because they’ll make your offense shine. It’s also a good idea to invest in training facilities and staff early on. Last but not least, don’t ignore your players' morale; happy players perform better and stay longer.

But unlike many sports games, Retro Bowl doesn’t overwhelm you with complicated mechanics. The controls are incredibly simple: just tap to run and swipe to throw. It’s easy to learn in just a few minutes, but it still offers enough challenge to keep you coming back.
