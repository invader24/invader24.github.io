About Monkey Mart

Ever dreamed of building and running your own supermarket? If so, Money Mart is exactly the game to try right now! In this idle game, you will take control of a cute monkey building your grocery store from the very beginning and grow it into a thriving supermarket empire in the jungle. Enjoy the game now!

What is Monkey Mart?

Monkey Mart is a charming and addictive idle management simulation game developed by TinyDobbins where you play as a hardworking monkey running a supermarket. Start as a humble banana shop; your goal is to plant crops, stock shelves, sell goods, and expand your market while investing coins earned through sales in unlocking new products and employees.

The store is getting more crowded as more customers come to shop. Try to serve them quickly and keep them satisfied. Upgrade your money character as well for better carrying capacity, speed, and energy. Overall, the whole gameplay is so thrilling and attractive that it may keep you hooked for hours.

How to play Monkey Mart game

Here's the main objective in Money Mart: grow your supermarket or grocery store from zero, make it more efficient, and unlock as many items and workers as possible while managing your monkey workforce and resources effectively. Ready? Visit https://monkeymart6x.github.io/ to play the game online for free. It's unblocked at school, work, and any other places.

Plant crops and harvest them

Start by planting bananas, harvesting them, and placing them on the shelves. Walk your character over to the planting area to automatically plant and harvest bananas. Then, carry the harvested ones to the nearby shelf to stock.

The first monkey customers will soon visit your store to buy your bananas, giving you the first revenue to invest in more crops and shelves. When you have enough money, you can plant corn, farm chickens for eggs, farm cows for milk, etc., so that the supermarket is filled with more products.

Serve the customers

Customers come in and pick up items from the shelves. Be sure to keep shelves stocked so that they don't have to wait long to pick the items they want. Once they’re done, they go to the checkout counter, and you must collect the coins they leave behind. Remember, when your store is expanded, there will be more customers visiting. Try to serve them fast and collect the money earned through sales. The more items you sell, the more coins you earn to upgrade and expand.

Hire monkey employees

As your market gets busier, you can hire helpers to automate tasks like harvesting crops, restocking shelves, feeding animals, and more. This helps keep everything running smoothly when many customers come at once, and multiple products are on shelves. Sometimes, your workers fall asleep; you need to wake them up to continue the work.

Upgrade your monkey character

With coins earned, you can upgrade your character to walk faster or carry more items. You can also upgrade your staff, the shelves, and other things in your mart. The more upgrades, the smoother, faster, and more effective your shop runs.

Game controls:

Use WASD or the arrow keys to control your monkey character’s movement.

Big Features That Make the Game a Hit

  • Simple yet extremely engaging idle management simulation gameplay.
  • Start with a fruit stand and grow into a supermarket empire.
  • Hire employees and boost your monkey’s speed and carrying capacity through upgrades.
  • Simple controls. Play with just a keyboard or touch.
  • Charming graphics, bright, cartoon-style graphics, and lovable monkey characters.
  • No game over or losing, just constant progress.

Ready to enjoy Monkey Mart?

Without a doubt, the cycle of planting, selling, upgrading, and expanding in Monkey Mart is deeply rewarding. It’s super easy to pick up and surprisingly addictive, and you’ll find yourself wanting to play more and more.
