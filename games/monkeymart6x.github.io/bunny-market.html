About Bunny Market

Bunny Market is an idle simulation game that is about guiding a little bunny to grow a vibrant marketplace. The game is set in a cozy, lush forest.

How to play Bunny Market

Build, sell, expand

Move your bunny around the forest (using arrow keys or WASD), chop trees for wood, collect stones or carrots, and use them to build stalls and unlock new areas.

Trading these goods at your market earns coins—your main growth engine.

Hire Helpers & Automate

As your stall grows, friendly NPC bunnies will arrive to help—for tasks like harvesting, trading, and expanding operations.

Expand the Market

Use your earnings to unlock more land, build more stalls, and diversify your product offerings. Your decisions—expand fast or optimize profits—shape your bunny empire.

Tips for players

Bunny Market was created by Angelo Studios. It features great idle simulation gameplay with various upgrades, nice graphics, and simple controls.

To win the game, you should focus on early resource loops. At the early stage, Chop wood, gather crops, and upgrade stalls early to unlock helpers—automation is key. Then, try to invest in stalls that increase trade volume or unlock more helpers.
